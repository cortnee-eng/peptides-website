About us

Peptide science,
explained honestly.

We exist because the peptide space desperately needs better information. Here's how we approach it.

Peptide Revealed exists because the peptide space desperately needs better information. Too much of what's available online is either vendor-funded marketing dressed up as education, anonymous posts with no sourcing, or fear-mongering content designed to drive clicks rather than inform.

We take a different approach. Every article we publish is grounded in peer-reviewed research, clearly cited, and written to help you understand what the evidence actually says — not what we want it to say.

Our editorial standards

We maintain strict editorial independence from peptide vendors, supplement companies, and pharmaceutical manufacturers. Our revenue comes from advertising and affiliate partnerships, but these relationships never influence our editorial content or recommendations.

When we reference research, we cite it. When the evidence is limited or conflicting, we say so. When something is animal-only research being marketed as proven in humans, we call that out.

What we cover

Our content spans the full spectrum of peptide research, including individual peptide profiles with mechanisms of action and safety data, regulatory and legal analysis, sourcing and quality evaluation guides, emerging research in longevity, cognitive, and metabolic peptides, and practical protocol information grounded in available evidence.

What we don't do

We don't provide medical advice. We don't make treatment recommendations. We don't sell peptides or any health products directly. If you're considering using any peptide, consult a qualified healthcare professional who can evaluate your specific situation, potential drug interactions, and help you weigh the risk-benefit ratio based on the available evidence.
